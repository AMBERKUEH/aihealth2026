✦ Core Features

Everything a caregiver needs, in one place.

From real-time safety alerts to AI-powered companionship, AmaCare is built for the full spectrum of dementia care.

🤖
AI Voice Companion
Engages patients in meaningful conversations, detects mood shifts, and flags concerns to caregivers automatically.
💊
Smart Medication Dispenser
Facial recognition ensures the right patient receives the right dose. Tracks adherence and triggers refill alerts.
📍
GPS Safety Wristband
Real-time location tracking with customizable geofence zones. Instant alerts when a patient steps outside boundaries.
🚨
SOS Emergency Function
One-press SOS on the wristband sends instant alerts to caregivers and emergency contacts with GPS coordinates.
📊
Caregiver Dashboard
A unified view of mood trends, medication logs, location history, and AI conversation summaries — all in one screen.
🧠
Mood & Health Insights
AI-generated weekly insights correlate mood, physical vitals, and behaviour patterns to surface early warning signs.
